Amazing Talks to Come

Every year we get two witness two weekends of General Conference. General Conference is when our church leaders dedicate time to speaking to us all live. Every session brings us talks that are always relevant to today's challenges, and gives us new tools on how to overcome the challenges. These talks are acting as a mouthpiece of God, allowing us to hear new modern day revelation. We are all very fortunate to have living prophets, seers and revelators.
