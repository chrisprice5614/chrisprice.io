Chris

Christian Price (or "Chris" as he usually goes by) is a computer scientist, composer and artist. Since the young age of 8, Chris has been fascinated with the idea of technology and computers, stemming from his enjoyment of playing video games. Video game development began as a hobby when he first programmed a "pac-man-esque" game at home while still only 8. Over the years, he refined his craft by developing 3D Multiplayer First Person Shooter games. Also while in middle school, Chris began learning piano and cello. Music and programming would eventually combine in college, where he would create digital soft-synths and music writing AI.
